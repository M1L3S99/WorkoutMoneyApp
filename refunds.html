Refunds & Cancellation

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Nature of charges

WorkoutMoney does not sell goods or subscriptions. The only charges are forfeits — amounts you expressly agreed to pay if you did not meet a workout commitment you signed. Because a forfeit is the result of the commitment you chose, correctly-calculated forfeits are generally non-refundable.

When we will refund

  • Errors: if you were charged due to a bug, duplicate charge, or a workout that was completed but not recorded, we will refund it in full. Contact us within 30 days of the charge.
  • Health exceptions: if you were charged for a period during which an approved health exception should have paused billing, we will refund the affected amount.
  • Unauthorised use: if a charge resulted from unauthorised access to your account, contact us and we will investigate and refund verified unauthorised charges.

How to request a refund

Email miles1chengscott@gmail.com with your account email and the date/amount of the charge. We aim to respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method via Stripe.

Cancellation

You can stop future commitments at any time by not signing a new contract and by logging out or deleting your account. An active commitment cannot be cancelled to avoid a legitimately owed forfeit, except via an approved health exception, which pauses billing.
