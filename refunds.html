Refunds & Cancellation

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Nature of charges

Ironbound does not sell subscriptions. It sells optional packs of premium gems as digital content. Older accounts may also have a legacy commitment charge until that commitment is removed from Profile.

When we will refund

  • Errors: if you were charged due to a bug, duplicate purchase, or incorrect legacy assessment, we will refund the affected amount. Contact us within 30 days of the charge.
  • Gem delivery: if payment succeeds but the purchased gems are not added to your account, we will add them or refund the charge.
  • Unauthorised use: if a charge resulted from unauthorised access to your account, contact us and we will investigate and refund verified unauthorised charges.

How to request a refund

Email miles1chengscott@gmail.com with your account email and the date/amount of the charge. We aim to respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method via Stripe.

Cancellation

There is no subscription to cancel. You can stop buying gems at any time. If an older Oath remains on your account, remove it from Profile to prevent future assessments.
