Commitment fitness

Put your money where your muscles are.

WorkoutMoney lets you stake your own money on a workout schedule you set. Show up and keep it all. Miss your sessions and you forfeit part of your stake — to a charity you choose. Loss aversion, turned into a personal trainer.

How it works

01

Set your schedule

Choose your exercises, rep targets and rest days for each day of the week.

02

Stake your money

Pick an amount to put on the line and add your card. Your card is saved securely with Stripe — we never see or store your card number.

03

Show up (or pay)

Complete your workouts, verified by the app's camera. Miss more than two non-rest days in a month and a portion of your stake is forfeited.

04

Forfeits go to good

Forfeited money is charged to your saved card and directed to the charity — or anti-charity — you chose when you signed.

Fair by design: rest days never count against you, you earn skip tokens over time to cover the occasional missed day, and a genuine health reason pauses all billing.

Pricing

WorkoutMoney is free to download and use. The only money involved is the stake you voluntarily choose when you sign a commitment contract — typically $25, $50, $100, $250, or a custom amount.

  • You are only ever charged if you break the commitment terms you agreed to.
  • More than two missed non-rest days in a month: one quarter of your stake is forfeited.
  • Rest days, days covered by skip tokens, and approved health exceptions never count against you.
  • Charges are made to the card you saved at signing, via Stripe.
