Push-up RPG

Your workout is the combat system.

Ironbound turns each camera-tracked push-up into an attack. Enter dungeons, defeat enemies, earn coins and experience, and build a stronger hero as your real-world training improves.

Open the app See how it works

How it works

01

Choose a dungeon

Compare its enemies, recommended strength, coin reward and experience reward.

02

Fight with push-ups

Your camera tracks form locally. Each completed rep damages the enemy before its next timed attack.

03

Improve your loadout

Spend earned gold on weapons, armour and charms, then equip them from Inventory.

04

Level up

Choose quests, build XP and unlock future rewards from the Great Hall.

Privacy by design: camera frames stay on your device and are not uploaded or stored by Ironbound.

Pricing

Ironbound is free to open and play. Optional premium gem packs can be purchased in the Market through Stripe.

  • Gem packs have fixed prices shown before you buy.
  • Dungeons reward coins and experience; premium gems are optional purchases.
  • Gems can be exchanged for gold; equipment itself is bought with gold.
  • Card details are handled securely by Stripe and never stored in this repository.
