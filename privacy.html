Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This policy explains what information Ironbound collects, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address and a password (stored securely by our authentication provider, Google Firebase).
  • Game and activity: completed push-ups, XP, dungeon runs, inventory, gold, gems, quests, and any legacy commitment history.
  • Payments: a payment token, customer ID and gem purchase record from Stripe. We never receive or store your full card number, CVC, or expiry — those are handled directly by Stripe.
  • Camera: pose landmarks are calculated in your browser. Camera video is not uploaded to or stored by Ironbound.
  • Technical: basic device/browser information needed to run the app.
  • Animation artwork: images opened in Sprite Lab are processed locally in your browser. They are not uploaded to us, Firebase, Stripe, or another server.

How we use it

  • To operate your account, game progression and workout tracking.
  • To process gem purchases and administer any older payment commitments, via Stripe.
  • To provide support and prevent abuse.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the processors that run the Service: Stripe (payments) and Google Firebase (authentication and database hosting). We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention & your rights

We keep your data while your account is active. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us. Deleting your account removes your workout and account data from our database; payment records may be retained by Stripe as required for financial/legal compliance.

Security

Data is stored on Google Firebase with access restricted to your own account. Payment credentials are tokenised by Stripe and never stored on our systems.

Children

The Service is not intended for anyone under 18.
