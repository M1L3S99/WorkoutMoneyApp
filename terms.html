1. What the Service does

WorkoutMoney is a voluntary commitment tool. You set a workout schedule and may choose to place a monetary stake on completing it. If you fail to meet the terms of a commitment you signed, a portion of your stake is forfeited and charged to your saved payment method.

2. Eligibility

3. Commitment contracts & charges

  • When you sign a contract you choose a stake amount and authorise us to charge your saved card for forfeits under the agreed rules.
  • Two consecutive missed scheduled days result in forfeiting half of your remaining balance. More than two missed non-rest days in a month result in forfeiting one quarter of your stake.
  • Rest days, days covered by earned skip tokens, and periods under an approved health exception do not count as missed.
  • Forfeited amounts are charged automatically to the card on file via our payment processor, Stripe, and directed to the recipient (charity or otherwise) you selected.
  • You authorise these charges to be made off-session (without further action from you) when a forfeit is owed.
